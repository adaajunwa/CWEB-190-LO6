Frequently Asked Questions

What does this site contain?

This site contains free educational web pages to help kids learn about letters and numbers. As time permits, we will add web pages about shapes and other topics.

What is the meaning of life?

Some people would answer that the "Answer to the Ultimate Question of Life, the Universe, and Everything" is 42. On the other hand, the Dalai Lama reportedly said the meaning of life is happiness, and that the real question is how you achieve happiness. Is it through wealth and possessions, or through caring and compassion for others? Is it being able to command others, or being in service to others?

How can we get in touch with you?

You can't. We are an anonymous collective that wants to protect our privacy, so we do not reveal any information about ourselves or solicit information from you. Please respect our privacy, just as we respect the privacy of our user's information.

Can I share or modify your web pages or code?

No.
